Our philosophy

🧈 Precision

We measure, test, and document. Pastry is chemistry — we embrace it with clarity and respect.

🌾 Curiosity

Why does butter behave this way? How does fermentation change flavor? We ask, then we bake.

🤝 Community

From home bakers to professionals, we share knowledge that elevates every kitchen.

Our story

  • 2020 Started as a small newsletter for pastry nerds — 200 subscribers.
  • 2021 First deep-dive guide on croissant lamination published; went viral in baking circles.
  • 2023 Launched the resource hub with calculators, charts, and downloadable checklists.
  • 2025 Expanded to video tutorials and partnered with artisan mills and chocolate makers.
  • 2026 Today: a global community of bakers, 40+ in-depth guides, and a shared love for the craft.

What we believe

Great pastry is not about perfection — it’s about understanding. We believe in demystifying technique so that every baker can make informed decisions. Whether you’re folding butter into dough for the first time or troubleshooting a ganache, we’re here with clear, tested advice.

We also believe in sustainability: sourcing transparently, reducing waste, and respecting the ingredients that make our craft possible.
