    Frequently asked questions

    Why does my croissant lose lamination?

    Butter temperature and dough relaxation are key. Ensure butter is pliable (around 14-16°C) and rest between folds.

    Can I substitute sourdough for commercial yeast in viennoiserie?

    Yes, but expect longer fermentation and adjust hydration. We have a full guide in our fermentation series.

    How do I prevent chocolate bloom?

    Proper tempering and storage at 18-20°C, away from moisture. Our tempering table gives exact cooling curves.
